MP3 players or Digital Audio Players are very special for me. Probably because they were the first devices 
that were personal to me and completely mine as a child.  

After not having a DAP for more than a decade, I recently purchased a Fiio M21 as an extension of my self-hosting setup, 
and I am quite happy with it. The gimmicky cassette case is actually useful for the Desktop mode and feels nice in the hands. 
I mostly use it while on my work desk or around the house when lounging. However, I did take it with me for a recent 
vacation and enjoyed going back to a wired setups (and not worrying about battery life). But to tell you the truth, 
you really cannot beat the convenience that the combination of wireless earbuds and phone bring while 
traveling - noise cancellation, great audio quality and no cables. Especially when you are traveling and have to take care 
of multiple things on you.

DAPs are a niche product for a very deliberate workflow around listening. Or, when you just want to disconnect from your phone. 
I like using one because it is a part of my self-hosting journey, and it lets me own my music listening experience.
